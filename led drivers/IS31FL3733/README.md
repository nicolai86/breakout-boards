### BOM

- 47nF 0805 Capacitor, 5x
- 100nF 0805 Capacitor, 4x
- 22uF 0805 Capacitor, 1x
- IS31FL3733 QFP-48, 1x
- 100k 0805 Resistor, 3x
- 20k 0805 Resistor, 1x
- 3528-RGB-LED, 64x

Optional, pinheaders for connectivity to other breakout boards
